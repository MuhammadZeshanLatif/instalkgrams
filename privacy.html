Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority

Last updated: January 15, 2024
Quick Summary: At InStalkgram, your privacy is our priority. We respect your privacy and limit data collection to a minimum. We do not collect personally identifiable information and do not store or track any Instagram content that you view or download.

1. Information We Collect

We respect your privacy and limit data collection to a minimum.

Personal Information

We do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email, or Instagram login credentials when you use our Instagram story viewing service.

Usage Data

We may collect anonymous usage data, such as:

  • Your device type and browser type
  • Session details and page views
  • Geographic location (country/region level only)
  • Referral sources

This data is used to improve our services and provide better experiences. We do not store or track any Instagram content that you view or download.

2. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience and gather anonymous data.

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly and cannot be disabled:

  • Session management cookies
  • Security cookies
  • Load balancing cookies

Analytics Cookies

We use Google Analytics to track usage patterns and improve our service. Google Analytics collects information like:

  • Session duration and page views
  • Geographic location (anonymized)
  • Device and browser information

Advertising Cookies

We work with third-party advertisers, including Google AdSense, to deliver personalized ads based on your interests. You can opt out of personalized ads by:

  • Adjusting your ad settings at www.google.com/settings/ads
  • Visiting www.aboutads.info for industry-wide opt-out options

3. Third-Party Services

InStalkgram may include links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the content or privacy policies of those sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services you access.

Instagram Content

Our service accesses publicly available Instagram content through official APIs and web scraping techniques. We do not store Instagram content on our servers. All content remains the property of Instagram and the original content creators.

4. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect to:

  • Improve our service: Analyze usage data to enhance user experience and website performance
  • Provide personalized content: Tailor advertisements based on your interests and browsing behavior
  • Ensure security: Protect against fraud, abuse, and security threats
  • Comply with legal obligations: Meet regulatory requirements and respond to legal requests

We do not collect sensitive personal data and do not engage in automated decision-making or profiling that would significantly affect you.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except in the following circumstances:

Service Providers

We may share anonymous data with trusted service providers who assist us in operating our website and conducting our business, such as:

  • Analytics providers (Google Analytics)
  • Advertising networks (Google AdSense)
  • Content delivery networks
  • Security services

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal processes or government requests
  • Protect our rights, property, or safety
  • Prevent fraud or security threats

6. Children's Privacy

InStalkgram is not intended for use by children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If we learn that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under 16, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

Parents or guardians who believe their child may have shared information with us can contact us at support@instalkgram.com to request its removal.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights related to your personal data:

Access and Portability

You can request access to the personal data we hold about you and receive a copy in a portable format.

Correction and Deletion

You may request to correct or delete any personal data we hold about you.

Opt-out of Advertising

You can opt out of personalized advertising by:

  • Adjusting your settings on Google Ads Settings
  • Using browser settings to block cookies
  • Visiting About Ads for industry opt-out tools

Withdraw Consent

Where we rely on your consent to process data, you can withdraw that consent at any time.

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Access controls and authentication systems
  • Data minimization practices

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. International Data Transfers

Our service may involve transferring data to countries outside your jurisdiction. When we transfer data internationally, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, such as:

  • Adequacy decisions by relevant authorities
  • Standard contractual clauses
  • Certification schemes

10. Data Retention

We retain data only for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. Specifically:

  • Usage data: Retained for up to 26 months for analytics purposes
  • Log files: Retained for up to 12 months for security and troubleshooting
  • Contact information: Retained only as long as necessary to respond to inquiries

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to check periodically for any changes.

If we make material changes to this policy, we will provide notice through our website or other appropriate means. By continuing to use InStalkgram after any changes are posted, you accept the revised terms.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or would like to exercise your rights, please contact us:

InStalkgram Privacy Team

Email: privacy@instalkgram.com

General Support: support@instalkgram.com

Response Time: We aim to respond to all privacy-related inquiries within 30 days.

13. Governing Law

This Privacy Policy is governed by the laws of the jurisdiction where our company is incorporated. Any disputes related to this policy will be resolved in the courts of that jurisdiction.

Your Privacy Matters: We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices. If you have any questions or concerns, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Questions About Our Privacy Policy?

Contact our privacy team for any questions or concerns about how we handle your data.
