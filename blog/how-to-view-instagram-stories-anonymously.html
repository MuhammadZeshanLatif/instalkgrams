Method 2: Browser Incognito Mode

While not completely anonymous, using incognito mode can provide an additional layer of privacy when browsing Instagram stories.

Browser Shortcuts:
  • Chrome: Ctrl+Shift+N (Windows) / Cmd+Shift+N (Mac)
  • Firefox: Ctrl+Shift+P (Windows) / Cmd+Shift+P (Mac)
  • Safari: Cmd+Shift+N (Mac)
Important Note

Incognito mode only prevents local storage of browsing data. Instagram can still track your IP address and device information.
